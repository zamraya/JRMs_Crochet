Shop Guidelines & Policies

We value transparency and kindness — here’s what to know before ordering from JRM’s Crochet.

All crochet pieces are handmade with care. Please allow enough time for crafting your order before shipment.

Payment Policy

Full payment or down payment is required upon confirmation of order to secure your slot.

Quality takes time. Kindly avoid rush requests to ensure each piece is crafted beautifully and neatly.

Return & Refund

Due to the handmade nature of products, returns and refunds are only accepted for damaged or incorrect items.
